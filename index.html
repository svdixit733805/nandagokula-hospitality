Who We Are

With decades of family experience in catering and a dedicated industrial kitchen, Nandagokula Hospitality operates with institutional discipline: documented SOPs, trained staff, and food-safety-first processes. We deliver consistent meals for 100s–1,200+ pax per service while adapting menus to nutritional and budget needs.

  • End-to-end operations: procurement → preparation → delivery
  • Food safety systems and trained supervisors on every shift
  • Menu planning with diet & budget options
  • On-site tasting sessions for decision-makers
